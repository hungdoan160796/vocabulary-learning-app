# Lesson: Talking About Food and Drinks You Like

<details>

**Barista:** Hi! What can I get for you today?
**Anna:** Hi! Can I get the... iced matcha latte you suggested last time? It's my favorite drink.
**Barista:** No problem. And what can I get for you today mister?
**Jack:** I'll have the black coffee, thanks.
**Barista:** Alright, one iced matcha latte and one black coffee coming right up.


**Jack:** So, iced matcha latte is pretty popular , huh?
**Anna:** Yeah it is. Black coffee is pretty... basic?
**Jack:** Yeah I know. I love the aroma of freshly brewed coffee every morning. I find that coffee helps me focus.
**Anna:** I like tea too. I think green tea is very refreshing. I prefer my tea with a little honey, instead of sugar.
**Jack:** Sounds delicious! Oh I don't just like coffees. I like to try different flavors when I visit new cafés.
**Anna:** Oh yeah? This place has very good Chai latte. Have you tried that?
**Jack:** Oh I don't like dishes or drinks that are spicy.
**Anna:** Oh it's not spicy at all. It's very balanced and mild.
**Jack:** Okay then, so that sounds good. I like dishes that are not too spicy.
**Anna:** Yeah you have to try it. I really enjoy the taste of it.

### Speaking Activity: Ordering and Talking About Drinks


**Useful Language:**  
- My favorite drink is…  
- I really enjoy the taste of…  
- I prefer… with…  
- I find that… helps me…  
- I like to try different flavors.  
- Could you recommend something?  
- Why do you like that drink?

### Listening Activity

John: Hey!
Lily: Hey! Long time no see. Great to see you again.
John: Me too. This place is really beautiful.
Lily: Yeah it is.
John: Have you tried any food here yet? 
Lily: Yes, I have. The sandwiches are really delicious. They are really fresh.  
John: That’s great! Besides sandwiches, what is your favorite dish?  
Lily: My favorite dish here is the fruit salad. I like it because it is sweet and healthy. How about you? What do you usually order at restaurants?
John: I can order anything, but I have to have potato chips. They taste crunchy and salty. I like them a lot.
Lily: I don’t like very salty food very much.
John: Oh, I understand. How about drinks? Do you like sweet drinks or sour ones?  
Lily: I usually like sweet drinks, like watermelon juice. Bitter drinks, like black coffee, are not my favorite. 
John: Yes, I know right. I remember trying this green juice once. It's called rau má. It's bitter, and I'm just not a fan.  
Lily: Same. I like to try new things, just not those.
