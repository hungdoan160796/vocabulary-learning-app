## New Vocabulary

| Vocabulary | Vietnamese Meaning | Example Sentence |
|------------|-------------------|------------------|
| friendly   | thân thiện        | My neighbor is very friendly and smiles a lot. |
| tall       | cao               | My cousin is tall with black hair. |
| short      | thấp, lùn         | She is short but very strong. |

## Dialogue

**At the neighborhood**

[audio:ctrang-lesson6-audio8]

<img src="/f64c2ed9-b28f-48bb-a388-52a4107dc215.png" width="500px"/>

<details>

**Liam:** Hi! Are you new in the neighborhood?  
**Bella:** Hello! Yes, I moved here last month. It’s nice to meet you.  
**Liam:** Nice to meet you too. Do you know Mr. Lee? He is a friendly neighbor.  
**Bella:** Yes, I met him last week. He is tall and kind. What about your family friends?  
**Liam:** I have a cousin who lives nearby. She is short and easygoing. I like her a lot.  
**Bella:** That sounds nice. I have a kind neighbor too. She always helps with house work.  
**Liam:** It is good to have friendly people around. Do you know that house over there?
**Bella:** Yes, Mrs. Brown lives in it. She is very kind and friendly. I like to talk with her.  
**Liam:** Me too! I feel happy to meet friendly people in our neighborhood.

</details>

### Dialogue Comprehension Questions

1. What does Liam say about his cousin?  
2. Why does Bella like her neighbor?

2. My cousin (live) near the park.  
3. She (not be) short; she is tall.
