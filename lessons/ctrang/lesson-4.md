2. Listening and Speaking: Make a reservation (15 minutes)
Vocabulary / Phrase	Example Sentence
Phone number	Can you give me your phone number?
Reservation	I made a reservation for 7 PM.
Appointment	I have an appointment at 3:30 PM.
Repeat	Could you repeat that, please?
Clarify	Please clarify the time of the meeting.
Confirm	Can you confirm the reservation details?
Book	I want to book a table for two.
Schedule	What is the schedule for tomorrow?
Dialogue 1

Details
Receptionist: Good afternoon, Dream Café. How can I help you? Caller: Hi, I’d like to reserve a table for two. Receptionist: Of course. May I have your phone number, please? Caller: Sure. It’s 213-555-6071. Receptionist: Let me repeat your number: 213-555-6071. Is that correct? Caller: Yes, that’s right. Receptionist: Your table is booked. See you soon!
Comprehension Questions

How many people is the reservation for?
What is the phone number given by the caller?
Did the receptionist repeat the number correctly?
Practice Exchange A: I'd like to reserve a table for three people. B: Certainly. What is your phone number? A: It’s 090-124-3685. B: 090-124-3685. Is that correct?

Dialogue 2

Details
Friend 1: Hi, are you free to meet this weekend? Friend 2: Yes! What day were you thinking? Friend 1: How about Sunday at 5:30 in the afternoon? Friend 2: At half past five? Friend 1: Yes, that's right. Does that work for you? Friend 2: Perfect! See you then.
Comprehension Questions

What day do they plan to meet?
At what time will they meet?
Who confirmed the time by repeating it?
Practice Exchange A: Let’s meet at 3:15. B: At quarter past three? A: Yes, see you then.
