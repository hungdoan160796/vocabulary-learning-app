
### Practice A: Fill in the Blank

1. There ___ a sofa and a table in the living room.  
2. The kitchen is ___ the living room.  
3. There ___ three chairs around the table.  

### Practice B: Rewrite the Sentence

1. Rewrite using *there is* or *there are*:  
   "A chair is near the sofa." → _________________________  
2. Change the preposition to *between*:  
   "The bathroom is next to the bedroom and kitchen." → _________________________  
3. Make plural and use *there are*:  
   "There is one chair in the kitchen." → _________________________  

**Answer Key:**  
1. There is a chair near the sofa.  
2. The bathroom is between the bedroom and kitchen.  
3. There are two chairs in the kitchen.

### Practice C: Complete the Conversation

1. A: "What rooms ___ in your home?"  
   B: "There ___ a living room and a kitchen."  

2. A: "Where ___ the sofa?"  
   B: "It ___ next to the window."  

3. A: "How many chairs ___ in the kitchen?"  
   B: "There ___ three chairs around the table."  

**Anna:** Hello! I’m Anna. I just moved in next door.  
**Linh:** Hi, Anna! I’m Linh. Welcome to the building!
**Anna:** Thank you. I'm very excided!
**Linh:** Good for you. Do you like your new home?  
**Anna:** Thank you! It’s nice. It has one living room, one kitchen, one bedroom, and one bathroom.  
**Linh:** Sounds great. What furniture do you have in your living room?  
**Anna:** There is a sofa, in front of the window, and a small table next to it. There are two chairs near the table.
**Linh:** Where is your kitchen?  
**Anna:** The kitchen is next to the living room. There is a big table and some chairs.
**Linh:** Great! Is your bedroom big?  
**Anna:** It’s not big, but there is a bed and a chair next to the window. I love the bed a lot. The bed is very comfortable.
**Linh:** Sounds lovely. I have a similar home. There is a bathroom between my bedroom and kitchen.
**Anna:** That’s convenient. I like my home because everything is so comfortable.
**Linh:** Me too! My home has many windows, so it is very bright.
