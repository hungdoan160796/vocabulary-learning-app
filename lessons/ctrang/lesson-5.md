### Listening Activity

**Context:**  
Jason meets Anna, a friendly woman from your neighborhood, at the local community garden event. You both start talking to get to know each other and share about your daily lives, families, and hobbies.

<img src="https://www.shutterstock.com/image-photo/teenage-friends-meeting-outdoor-guy-260nw-2358760203.jpg"/>

**Dialogue:**

[audio:ctrang-lesson5-audio1]

<details>

**Jason:** Hi! Great to meet you. I’m Jason.
**Anna:** Hi Jason, I’m Anna. It’s nice to meet you!  
**Jason:** Nice to meet you too! So, do you live close to here?  
**Anna:** Yes, I live close to the community center, so I often go to these local events. How about you?  
**Jason:** I live just down the street from here.
**Anna:** Is that so! Um do you want some coffee?
**Jason:** I had coffee already, thanks. I usually start my day with a cup of coffee and reading the news.
**Anna:** Oh I usually start my day by going for a walk. There's this park near my house. It helps me relax. I enjoy taking walks there when I have some time.
**Jason:** That sounds lovely. Don't you have any family member to go with?
**Anna:** There's my parents, my sister, and me, and they honestly prefer staying at home. We often spend weekends visiting other relatives.  
**Jason:** I see. I take that you also have a very athletic job?
**Anna:** No, not at all. I'm working in marketing, as an assistant. I am responsible for managing the office schedule and appointments. And you?
**Jason:** I work as a graphic designer. I prefer work that involves creativity.  
**Anna:** That must be fun! Being creative is awesome. I love music. I sometimes relax by listening to music.  
**Jason:** Same! Oh that, and reading books. I often take breaks to clear my mind, and listen to some music.
**Anna:** That’s great. Lunch break is all I have. I usually go to the nearby café with my colleagues and have lunch there. What do you usually do for lunch?  
**Jason:** I usually eat at home, but sometimes I join my coworkers. 
**Anna:** Oh so you just come home from work, eat lunch, and then go back to work?
**Jason:** Oh no not like that. I commute by bus, which takes about 30 minutes each day. I usually work remote in the morning, have lunch, then come to work.
**Anna:** Lucky you!
**Jason:** Haha I get that a lot. Oh I think I gotta go now. It was really nice talking with you today. I really enjoy meeting new people and making friends.
I like meeting new people because it helps me make new friends as well. 
**Anna:** Same! See ya!
</details>

### Comprehension Questions

1. Where does Anna live?  
2. How does Jason start his day?  
3. What does Jason enjoy in his free time?  
4. Who is in Anna’s family?  
5. What is Jason’s job?  
6. How do both people go to work?  
7. What do both speakers like about meeting new people?
