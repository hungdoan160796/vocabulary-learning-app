# Introducing Yourself — Name, Age, Hometown

## Lesson Objectives

* Learn and practice natural self-introduction phrases (name, age, hometown)
* Build confidence and reflex in speaking short sentences about yourself
* Recognize and understand introductions in spoken English
* Use self-introductions to start conversations in informal settings

# Lesson Outline

### 1. Introducing Yourself

**Q: What’s your name?** => **My name is __.**

**Q: How old are you?** => **I am __ years old.**/**I am in my thirties.**

**Q: Where are you from?**/**Where do you live?** => **I live in __ city/district.** / **I live on __ street.**

**Q: What is your job/profession?** => **I currently work as __** / **I am a __**
