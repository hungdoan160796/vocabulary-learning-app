## Making and Confirming Reservations

Complete the conversations below by filling in the missing lines. Use the words from the vocabulary list to help you.

restaurant, reservation, appointment, schedule, book, reserve, booked, confirm, details

### Conversation 1: Making a Reservation at a Restaurant

A: Hello! I would like to ___ a table for four at your ___.

B: Sure! When would you like to come?

A: Tomorrow at 7 pm. Can you ___ the booking?

B: Yes, your ___ is confirmed for tomorrow at 7 pm.

### Conversation 2: Booking an Appointment

A: Hi, I need to ___ an ___ with the doctor.

B: What day works best for you?

A: Friday morning, please. Can you check the doctor's ___ for me?

B: Yes, we have 9 am or 10 am available. Which one do you prefer?

A: 9 am, please. Can you ___ the ___?

B: Done! Your appointment is ___ for Friday at 9 am.
